Why membership matters

The BVI tourism industry is strongest
when it moves as one.

One voice is ignored. Many voices are noise. One united voice is power.

Right now, BVI tourism businesses lobby individually — or not at all. Policies get set, fees get levied, and infrastructure decisions get made without organised industry input. Not because Government doesn't care, but because no one is speaking for the sector as a whole. BVIYHTA is that voice.

Your real competition isn't down the road. It's unrepresented policy.

The restaurant nearby isn't your enemy — a licensing change that hurts you both is. The charter company at the next dock isn't your rival — a customs policy that drives visitors to the USVI is. BVIYHTA turns shared frustrations into shared solutions, and shared solutions into real change.

Every member amplifies every other member.

An association's power is directly proportional to its membership. Every business that joins — from a solo taxi driver to a 200-room resort — adds weight to every conversation BVIYHTA has on your behalf. Your membership doesn't just benefit you. It strengthens the entire BVI tourism industry.

"A taxi driver, a hotel manager, a dive instructor, and a restaurant owner approach Government — separately, they wait outside. Together, as BVIYHTA members, they're called in first."
Who we represent

If tourism touches your business, BVIYHTA is your association.

We represent the full spectrum of BVI tourism and hospitality — not just the obvious players, but every business that depends on visitors to these islands.

🏨

Hotels, Resorts & Villas

From boutique guesthouses to full-service resorts and private villa rentals — the foundation of BVI land-based tourism.

⚓

Marine Tourism

Bareboat & crewed charters, water taxis, ferry operators, dive shops, snorkel tours, fishing charters, boatyards & chandleries.

🍽️

Restaurants & Bars

Beach bars, fine dining, dock-side restaurants, beach clubs — the experiences visitors tell everyone about when they get home.

🚕

Taxis & Transport

Taxi drivers, car rental companies, and private transfer services — the first face every visitor sees.

🤿

Tours & Activities

Excursion operators, hiking guides, water sports, kitesurfing, kayaking — the adventures that define a BVI visit.

🛍️

Retail & Provisioning

Supermarkets, chandleries, gift shops, local craft vendors, and provisioning services that keep visitors — and charter fleets — stocked.

Allied Members — accountants, lawyers, cleaning contractors, staffing agencies, builders, IT providers, and any business that works in support of the tourism industry — are warmly welcomed. If tourism thrives, so do you. BVIYHTA recognises that, and so should your membership.
Member benefits

What your membership delivers.

Practical, meaningful value — from your first day as a member.

01

A seat at the table

Direct representation in tourism policy discussions with Government and the BVI Tourist Board. Your interests are heard before decisions are made — not after.

02

Input on policy & regulation

Provide structured feedback on licensing, taxation, infrastructure, import duties, work permits, and any regulation that affects your bottom line.

03

Training & professional development

Access hospitality training resources, workshops, and certification programmes that elevate your team's skills and your business's reputation.

04

Marketing & visibility

Feature in BVIYHTA's marketing materials and member directory — reaching visitors planning their BVI trip before they even arrive.

05

Tourism intelligence

Stay informed on cruise schedules, upcoming events, resort developments, and destination strategy — information that helps you plan, staff, and grow your business.

06

Community & network

Connect with fellow BVI tourism businesses, collaborate on shared challenges, and be part of a community committed to sustainable, responsible tourism development.
